Revolution of Componenets

A personal computer (PC) is made up of several essential components, each serving a specific role to ensure smooth operation. At its core is the central processing unit (CPU), which acts as the brain, executing commands and calculations. The motherboard serves as the foundation, connecting all parts and facilitating communication between them.

The CPU is the brain of your computer, responsible for executing instructions and performing calculations. Key factors to consider when choosing a CPU include core count, clock speed, and compatibility with your motherboard.

The GPU handles rendering images, videos, and games, making it essential for gaming, video editing, and 3D rendering. Look for factors like VRAM capacity, clock speed, and support for technologies like ray tracing.
